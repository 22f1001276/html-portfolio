The Best Movies According to Kush

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. Starring Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hath away, Jessica Chastain, Bill Irwin, Ellen Burstyn, and Michael Caine, the film features a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity.

Sanam Teri Kasam

Sanam Teri Kasam is a 2016 Indian romantic tragedy film directed by Radhika Rao and Vinay Sapru. Starring Harshvardhan Rane and Mawra Hocane, the film is a modern rendition of the novel "Love Story" by Eric Segal. It tells the story of a couple who fall in love despite the odds and societal pressures.

KGF Chapter 1

KGF Chapter 1 is a 2018 Indian Kannada-language period action film directed by Prashanth Neel. It stars Yash in the lead role as Rocky, alongside Srinidhi Shetty, Ramachandra Raju, and Anant Nag. The film is set in the 1970s and follows Rocky's journey from poverty to power in the Kolar Gold Fields. It is known for its high-octane action sequences and gripping storyline.
